About us

We are excited to introduce our organisation Mountain Traveller (Mountain Traveller), which specialises in organising experience-based Treks and Experiential Stays. We were formed to address the adventure needs of Trekking enthusiasts, Hikers and Backpackers wanting to explore the raw and unadulterated India, especially in Uttarakhand and Himachal Pradesh. Our organisation is based in Uttarakhand - India. We offer over-all logistics when it comes to Trekking and Outdoors in Uttarakhand and other Himalayan states.
